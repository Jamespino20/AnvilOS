Frequently Asked Questions

How does the offline mode actually work? expand_more

AnvilOS uses a local-first architecture. If the connection drops, your register continues processing sales locally using cached inventory data. Once reconnected, it seamlessly syncs all transactions and updates back to the central server without requiring manual intervention.

Will it work with my existing hardware? expand_more

Yes. AnvilOS is designed to be hardware-agnostic. It supports most standard USB and Bluetooth barcode scanners, receipt printers (Epson, Star Micronics), and cash drawers. We provide a compatibility checking tool to verify your specific setup.

Can you help migrate data from our old POS? expand_more

Absolutely. We offer data migration services for Pro and Enterprise plans. Our onboarding team will map and import your existing customer lists, inventory databases (including historical cost data), and supplier information.

How long does it take to train staff? expand_more

The register interface is designed to be learned in under 15 minutes. Back-office management training typically takes 1-2 hours. We provide extensive documentation and video tutorials for all core functions.
